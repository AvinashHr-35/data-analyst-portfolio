##  Project 2 — Bank Customer exit & Revenue Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed 10,000 bank customers to identify exit patterns and revenue at risk.
Goal: Find which customers are leaving and why — then recommend retention strategies.

## Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Excel | Data audit & conditional formatting |
| SQL (SQLite) | 8 business queries + revenue analysis |
| Python | EDA, 8 visualizations |
| Power BI | Interactive dashboard |

## Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle — Bank Customer exit Prediction
- **Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shubh0799/churn-modelling
- **Size:** 10,000 rows, 14 columns

## Key Findings
1. **20.37%** customers left the bank
2. **Germany** has 32.44% exit — 2x higher than France/Spain
3. **51-60 age group** has 56.21% exit — highest risk
4. **2 products** = lowest exit (7.58%); **4 products** = 100% exit
5. **Inactive members** exit 2x more than active members
6. **Female customers** exit more than males in all 3 countries
7. **718 high-value customers** lost — Balance 100K+, Age 41-60
8. **6.75 Crore revenue at risk** from 1-product high-value customers

## Business Recommendations
- Investigate Germany operations — local service or competitor issue
- Launch loyalty program for 51-60 age group
- Offer 2nd product to 1-product customers — reduces exit by 73%
- Re-engagement campaign for inactive members
- Female-targeted retention offers — especially in Germany
